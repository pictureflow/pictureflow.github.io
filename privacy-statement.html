Gathering personal data

Our application and website do not store your personal data. It’s impossible. We have no means of collecting it and no where to store it. So even if Elon Musk or Batman asked nicely we wouldn’t have anything to show them.
The only way for us to collect some of your personal data is when you would send an email to us. When you send us an email, we are only interested in collecting general contact information, such as your name and email address. And we want to encourage you to not share any personal data not absolutely necessary.

Storing personal data

We will store the email-correspondence and the personal data it contains as long as it is part of an active conversation. If we have not heard back from you in 1 months time, we will anonymise any feedback and delete all emails. This includes screenshots or pictures in which people are recognisable.
